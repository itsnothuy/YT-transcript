Privacy Policy for YouTube Transcript Downloader

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not track your browsing history, video views, or any other personal information.

Permissions Explained

Our extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • downloads: Required to save transcript files to your local computer. Files are saved directly to your Downloads folder.
  • webRequest: Used to intercept YouTube's caption requests for reliable transcript downloads. We only intercept requests to youtube.com/api/timedtext (caption endpoint). No other web requests are monitored or intercepted.
  • youtube.com access: Required to access YouTube video pages and detect available caption tracks.

Data Usage

All transcript processing happens locally in your browser. The extension:

  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not store any data beyond the current browser session
  • Does not track or log your activity
  • Only processes caption data when you explicitly click the "Download transcript" button

How It Works

When you use this extension:

  1. You click the "Download transcript" button on a YouTube video
  2. You turn on captions manually by clicking the CC button
  3. The extension intercepts YouTube's own caption request (which YouTube makes when you turn on captions)
  4. The caption data is processed locally in your browser
  5. A text file is saved to your Downloads folder
  6. No data is transmitted to any external servers

Third-Party Services

This extension only interacts with YouTube.com to download publicly available caption data. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the complete source code and verify our privacy claims on GitHub:

https://github.com/itsnothuy/YT-transcript

Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and post the updated policy on our GitHub repository. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. The extension is completely privacy-preserving by design.
